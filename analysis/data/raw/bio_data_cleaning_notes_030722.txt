Data Cleaning Notes

FISH

Fish samples were excluded if:
- Reach length less than 25 meters 
- Less than 10 individuals in small streams
- Less than 20 individuals in mid-large streams
- Greater than 250 M reach in small streams
- Greater than 500 M reach in mid-large streams
- Older Pomperaug basin samples with slimy sculpin than may have been knobfin sculpin 

If multiple samples were collected in a single year one was randomly chosen

BUGS

Only first field duplicate or lab subsample rep was used in the analysis
If multiple samples were collected in a single year one was randomly chosen
Only kicknet sample method samples used
Samples with less than 100 individuals were excluded
Only fall samples were used
